SOC Analyst Overview

SOC Analysts serve as the first line of defense within a Security Operations Center. They monitor security tools, review alerts, and investigate potential threats to protect organizational systems and data. Entry-level analysts support threat detection efforts while gaining experience in incident response and established security workflows (Radiant Security, 2025).

SOC Analyst Responsibilities

  • Monitor security alerts and SIEM dashboards
  • Investigate suspicious activity and analyze logs
  • Identify indicators of compromise (IOCs)
  • Escalate confirmed incidents to senior analysts
  • Assist with incident response procedures
  • Document findings and maintain security reports
